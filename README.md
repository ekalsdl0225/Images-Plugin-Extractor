Original plugin at: https://github.com/Andavin/Images

How to use:

You must place the images.db (Stored in SQLite format; plans for adding others in the future are undecided) and usercache.json (Optional, to tag who uploaded the image) files at /Users/username/Desktop on Mac or C:\Users\user\Desktop on Windows.

Next, you can extract the images by running Images-Plugin-Extractor.

Because an algorithm was used to guess the horizontal and vertical orientation of the images, some images may be saved corrupted.
